Vegetarian Chilli


Meatless Chilli Recipe

Here is how to make the perfect vegetarian chilli. You won't miss the meat.

    Ingredients
  • 1 small onion and carrot
  • 1 med green pepper
  • 1 frozen bag corn
  • Canned black and red kidney beans
  • Canned tomato sauce
  • Chilli powder and cumin
  • Shredded cheese and hot sauce for topping
  • Salt and pepper
    Steps
  1. Chop up veggies.
  2. Cook veggies until tender.
  3. Drain canned beans and add to pot.
  4. Add chilli powder and cumin.
  5. Add canned tomato sauce and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Let simmer for about 20-30 minutes.
