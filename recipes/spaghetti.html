Spaghetti

Description

Meatless spaghetti.

    Ingredients
  • small onion, whatever veggies
  • canned lentils
  • tomato sauce
  • packaged spaghetti
  • herbs
  • salt & pepper
    Steps
  1. Chop up small onion and veggies.
  2. Cook until browned.
  3. Add canned lentils, herbs, salt & pepper.
  4. Add tomato sauce
  5. In another pot, add your pasta to boiling water. Follow the direction on package.
  6. Combine and enjoy.
