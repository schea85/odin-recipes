Vegetarian Tacos


Meatless Tacos Recipe

Here is how to make the perfect vegetarian tacos. You won't miss the meat.

    Ingredients
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 med green pepper
  • Portobello mushroom
  • Soft Tortillas
  • Canned black beans
  • Chilli powder and cumin
  • Shredded cheese and hot sauce for topping
  • Salt and pepper
    Steps
  1. Slice onion, green pepper, and portobello mushroom
  2. Cook veggies until tender.
  3. Drain canned beans and set aside.
  4. Season veggie with chilli powder and cumin.
  5. Warm up tortillas.
  6. Assemble tacos with cooked veggies, black beans, and whatever toppings
